About

A satellite event of the 11th International Conference on Industrial Ecology

The purpose of the Creative Abstract Competition is to encourage researchers who are presenting at the ISIE 2023 to think about their research creatively and attempt to communicate it in a new way.

The competition is bring organised by researchers from the Leiden University Centre for Environmental Sciences (CML)

The competition will open for entries between the 2nd and 23rd of June the winners elected by the ISIE attendees during the conference
